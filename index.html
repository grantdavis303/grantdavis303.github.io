About Me

Hi everyone, my name is Grant!

As of right now, I am a student at Turing, a software engineering bootcamp based out of Denver, CO. Before Turing, I worked as the Marketing and Communications Coordinator for a non-profit in the electrical industry.

My previous job helped me develop a lot of great skills, including effective communication, collaboration, and time management. With the projects I was responsible for, I found what helped me the most was creating a plan for how I would tackle the project in the beginning, as well as asking for a lot of feedback, allowing me to refine the project and provide the best deliverable I could.

After about 5 years, from my internship to working full-time, I thought it was time for a change, and computers and software have always been interesting to me. Even though I had experience in marketing and design, I chose to pursue backend software engineering because it was completely new and unknown, and I was excited about that challenge.
