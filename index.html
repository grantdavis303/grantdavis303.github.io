About Me

My journey in tech began in 2010 with my very first lines of code, creating basic HTML and CSS web pages in Windows Notepad. After moving to Colorado in 2011, I took a creative detour into photography, graphic design, and video production. This new passion for visual storytelling, combined with a business mindset, led me to graduate from CU Boulder with a degree in Strategic Communication.

In 2021, I stepped into the role of Marketing Coordinator for a local non-profit in the electrical industry. While I really enjoyed marketing, my love for coding never faded. In 2024, I embraced the challenge of becoming a backend engineer by enrolling in Turing, a software engineering boot camp based in Denver.

Throughout my time at Turing, I collaborated with diverse teams to build and deploy impactful projects. I enjoy working with relational databases, developing REST APIs, and creating dynamic web applications. My current technical toolkit includes Ruby, Ruby on Rails, and SQL, and I am excited to expand my skills with new languages and tools like Python, Go, Docker, and Terraform.

I am currently looking to join a team where I can continue to learn, grow, and contribute. If you are interested in discussing my career journey, new or emerging tech, or just want to chat, please feel free to reach out!
